The Atelier

Crafted in small batches,
with intention

Flour & Ferment Atelier is a pre-order microbakery built on a simple belief: the best food starts with the best ingredients and the patience to let them do their work. Every item is made from scratch using all organic ingredients — no shortcuts, no fillers, no compromises.

From slow-fermented sourdough to hand-crafted brie and curated charcuterie, each offering is a small act of craft. We bake and create in limited quantities, so every order gets the attention it deserves.

What We Make

From our atelier to your table

01

Sourdough Bread

Long-fermented, naturally leavened loaves in rotating varieties — plain, jalapeño cheddar, everything bagel, cinnamon sugar, and more.

02

Cookies

Small-batch cookies in seasonal flavors — from classic chocolate chip to matcha strawberry. Organic butter, real vanilla, crisp edges, soft centers.

03

Custom Brie

Hand-crafted brie cheeses made to order. Creamy, bloomy-rind perfection — customized with herbs, honey, or seasonal infusions.

04

Compound Butter

Organic butter blended with fresh herbs, roasted garlic, edible flowers, or seasonal flavors. The finishing touch for any table.

05

Charcuterie Cups

Individually portioned cups of curated meats, cheeses, fruits, and accompaniments. Perfect for gatherings, gifts, or solo indulgence.

06

Charcuterie Boards

Full-size boards with artisan meats, house-made brie and compound butter, seasonal fruits, and thoughtful pairings.
